# 🧸 Mini Tamagotchi Game Design

A browser-based virtual pet game built with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript using Pixelify Sans.

Font: https://fonts.google.com/specimen/Pixelify+Sans

---

## 🎮 Core Concept

A retro-style Tamagotchi device where you raise Pokémon-like creatures from an egg through multiple life stages.

Each playthrough randomly selects one of:
- Wooper
- Mienfoo
- Cherubi
- Sentret
- Cherrim (special behavior form system)

All Pokémon start as a **white egg** and evolve through care-based progression.

---

## 🎨 Visual Style

- Muted retro aesthetic (slightly desaturated, aged plastic look)
- Round handheld device
- 3 buttons: A / B / C
- Movable keychain (purely cosmetic, no gameplay effect)
- Pixel-style black & white screen (soft monochrome, not harsh white)

### Device Color Themes (change per Pokémon)

#### Wooper
- Shell: muted dusty blue
- Buttons: soft periwinkle

#### Mienfoo
- Shell: muted berry red
- Buttons: warm cream

#### Cherubi
- Shell: softened red-pink
- Buttons: muted avocado green

#### Sentret
- Shell: muted caramel brown
- Buttons: soft sand beige

---

## 🐣 Growth System

All Pokémon begin as a **white egg**.

They evolve through:
- Stage 1
- Stage 2
- Final Stage

After reaching final stage and interacting for 5 minutes, the player can choose to switch to a new Pokémon.

---

## 🍦 Food System

- Stage 1 food: Ice Cream 🍦
- Stage 2 food: Sundaes 🍨

---

## 📊 Stats System

Each Pokémon has:
- Hunger
- Energy
- Happiness

Stats change over time with a slow, chill decay system.

---

## ❤️ Interaction System

### Love Button
- Increases happiness
- Triggers floating heart animations

### Sleep Function
- Restores energy
- Counts toward evolution requirements

---

## 💩 Care System

- Poop appears after 5 feedings
- Must be cleaned to progress evolution

---

## 📈 Evolution Requirements (Stage 1 → Stage 2)

To evolve, ALL must be completed:
- Feed 5 times
- Clean poop 1 time
- Sleep 2 times (each sleep lasts ~10 seconds)
- Press love button 4 times

---

## ⚠️ Neglect System

Neglect is defined by:
- No interaction over time
- AND/OR low stat conditions

### Consequences:
- 10 minutes no care → Pokémon runs away (warning state first)
- 12 hours no care → Pokémon dies (blinking warning first)

---

## 💀 Death Screen

When a Pokémon dies:

> "_____ (Pokémon) lived for _____ minutes. _____ (Pokémon) was loved by all."

Then a new egg Pokémon is generated.

---

## 🌸 Cherrim Special System

Cherrim has two final forms based on care quality:

### 🌞 Sunny Form
- Triggered if Cherrim had a **very loved childhood**
- Condition: high happiness / frequent interaction above required threshold

### ☁️ Overcast Form
- Triggered if Cherrim was **barely cared for but still raised**
- Condition: low-to-average care history

This creates a hidden “childhood quality” system affecting final evolution appearance.

---

## 🔄 Final Stage System

- After reaching final evolution:
  - 5 minutes of interaction unlocks option to switch to a new Pokémon

---

## 🧠 Core Design Philosophy

- Chill idle gameplay
- Emotional feedback through animations
- Simple stat management
- Long-term progression loop
- Replayable with different Pokémon personalities
